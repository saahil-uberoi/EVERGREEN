About Grasslands

Grasslands is a vegetation area dominated by almost continuously covered grass and it is alsoone of the most widely distributed main vegetation types in the world.

The Victorian Volcanic Plains

The natural temperate grassland of VVP is highly fragmented and critically endangered ecological community.

Threats to Biodiversity in Victoria Volcanic Plains

The interactions between native species are altered or destroyed by these invasive species, and climate change, Pollution, Overexploitation, and deforestation are also the major cause of extinction and can result in the loss of native biodiversity. The fewer species we have on the earth, the fewer humans we will have.
